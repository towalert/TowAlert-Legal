Privacy Policy for TowAlert

Last updated: June 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Account Information
When you register, we collect your email address and display name to create and manage your account.

Location Data
If you enable "Use My Location," we access your device's GPS coordinates solely to show nearby incidents on the map. We do not store your location on our servers. Map functionality is powered by Google Maps, which may independently process location data per Google's Privacy Policy.

Push Notification Tokens
We collect your device's push notification token (FCM token on Android, APNs token on iOS) to send you real-time traffic incident alerts. This token is stored on our servers and linked to your account. It is deleted when you sign out or uninstall the app.

Device Information
We collect basic device identifiers including device type and operating system version for the purpose of routing push notifications and diagnosing technical issues.

IP Addresses
Our backend servers automatically log your IP address with each request. This data is used solely for security monitoring and is not linked to your profile.

Usage Data
We collect basic app usage data (incident views, filter preferences) stored locally on your device via AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and authenticate your account
  • To send you push notifications for nearby traffic incidents
  • To display location-relevant content on the live map
  • To manage your subscription status
  • To diagnose technical issues and monitor for abuse

3. Subscription & Payments

TowAlert offers a Premium subscription at $30/month with a 7-day free trial. Payments are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not store your payment information. Subscription management and cancellation is handled through your device's App Store or Google Play account settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and user management
  • Google Maps — map rendering and location display
  • Render — backend hosting (incident data, push tokens)
  • Apple / Google — payment processing for subscriptions

5. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. Push tokens are deleted when you sign out or uninstall the app. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us.

6. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Firebase Authentication secures your login. We enforce single-device sessions — logging in on a new device automatically signs out your previous device.

7. Children's Privacy

TowAlert is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Your Rights

  • Access or delete your account at any time
  • Disable push notifications in your device settings
  • Disable location access in your device settings

towalertsupport@gmail.com

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
