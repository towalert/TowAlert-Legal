Account Deletion – TowAlert

Last updated: June 4, 2026

TowAlert respects your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to delete your account and what happens to your data when you do.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly inside the app at any time:

  1. Open the TowAlert app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Go to Your Username
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your choice when prompted

Your account will be permanently deleted immediately. This action cannot be undone.

Request Deletion by Email

If you are unable to access the app or prefer to request deletion by email, contact us at:
towalertsupport@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your TowAlert account in your request. We will process your deletion within 30 days of receiving your request and confirm by email once complete.

What Data Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your account information (name, email address, password)
  • Your saved incident history and alert preferences
  • Your push notification tokens
  • Your subscription and payment records held by TowAlert
  • Any other data associated with your account

Please note that payment transaction records held by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play) are subject to their own data retention policies and are not controlled by TowAlert.

Active Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active subscription. Before deleting your account, please cancel your subscription through:

  • iPhone/iPad: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → TowAlert → Cancel
  • Android: Google Play Store → Subscriptions → TowAlert → Cancel

We do not offer refunds for unused portions of a billing period. For questions about billing, contact us at towalertsupport@gmail.com.
